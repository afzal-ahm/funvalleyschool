About Ourselves

Fun Valley (A Pre-School), is one of the most trusted early childhood education care center with modern teaching standards.

Established with a motto to give a strong foundation to the child using a combination of the most modern educational techniques along with showering love and providing motherly care to each individual.

Fun Valley Team, offer a different approach to academic excellence for children aged 2 onwards.

We nurture talent, collaborate duality and develop curious, inquisitive and constantly challenging minds.

Apart from being an educationist, he is a Philanthropist and constantly promoting human welfare by contributing generously to uplift the underprivileged people of our society.

Under the guidance of Chairman, Mr Rajesh Jain the school is running successfully. He has played a significant role in the field of education renaissance and in building mental and physical development of the students.

Our Aim

Our aim is to make a strong foundation of the little ones by developing the overall personality to bring confidence, ability to communicate and express themselves freely. We believe that every child should have the opportunity to experience the joy of success. We are totally committed to building a healthier future. This we develop by maturing the social-emotional, physical, intellectual, creative and language abilities in the child.

Our Curriculum

Computer Education

Our computer classes will fully prepare your child for today's technology environment.

Music Programs

Music ignites all areas of child development and skills for school readiness, including intellectual, social-emotional, motor, language, and overall literacy. AsIt helps the body and the mind work together.

Dance Class

Dance has a major role in boosting your child's social development. When children dance, they express their emotions in front of their audience. With all that grooving, a child feels safe to open up to the world.

Art and Craft Classes

Art and craft activities give kids a sense of achievement and allow them to take pride in their work which builds confidence. Making art is a great, safe way to discover that it's okay to make mistakes and that getting things 'wrong' can lead you to a whole new idea.

Sports Programs

Taking part in sports is important for children as it reduces stress and enhances their mood. Sports builds healthy bones and muscles, increases fitness, improves sleep, helps them socialize. It also improves their cooperation skills, boosts self-confidence, and lowers the risk of getting obese.

Personality Development

All the activities including stage presentation, participation in annual function helps in building all-round personaloty of the child.
