Sports Program:

Taking part in sports is important for children as it reduces stress and enhances their mood. Sports builds healthy bones and muscles, increases fitness, improves sleep, helps them socialize. It also improves their cooperation skills, boosts self-confidence, and lowers the risk of getting obese.

Fun Valley prepares children for competitions on and off the field as opportunities to learn from their success and failure. In addition, losing often motivates kids to work even harder for next time. They learn to respect authority, rules, team colleagues and opponents. Sport is an important learning environment for children.

Fun Valley also organises “Annual Sports Day”. Parents are not only invited but they are also made to participate in the event with their children. And at the end of the event we always have a bunch of Happy Parents with Happy Children.

Taekwondo:

Taekwondo helps kids become more physically active and helps the body in various ways, such as improving flexibility, cardio, and improving blood circulation. Additionally, physical activity releases dopamine, a hormone that enhances positive emotions, which can help the mental health of kids.

Taekwondo classes are conducted once a week by our professional trained instructor. Children also demonstrate their act in Annual Function of the School.

HORSE RIDING:

As children love animals therefore Fun Valley has scheduled a weekly Horse Riding Class for all the students.

It teaches a child to put the needs of the horse and therefore others before their own and develops compassion and empathy. They will learn the importance of discipline, respect and a range of additional skills in a caring and nurturing way.
